About our NGO

At viveHOY we are a non-profit organization founded in 2026 with the aim of fighting the housing crisis suffered by young people in Spain. We are a group of young professionals, volunteers and activists committed to the right to decent housing.

What do we do?

Legal Advice

Legal advice for rentals and grants.

Youth Housing

Youth housing board with affordable prices.

Awareness

Awareness campaigns and political pressure.
