Projects



Conference at the Jesuïtes Bellvitge - Joan XXIII institute

Conference at the Jesuïtes Bellvitge - Joan XXIII institute for Business Administration and Management (ADE) students about young people's accommodation and the right to decent housing.


Presence at the Parliament of Catalonia

We went to the Parliament of Catalonia to raise awareness among parliamentary politicians and those present about the current housing problem and the difficulty for young people to access it.


Fundraising in collaboration with the NGO "La Vinya"

Collection of food and donations to help homeless people.


Conference at the Abat Oliba i Loreto institute.

A conference was held at the Abat Oliba i Loreto institute for 4th year of ESO, 1st and 2nd year of Batxillerat students about young people's accommodation and the right to decent housing.


Financial orientation class at Centre Educatiu Esclat.

A financial orientation class was held at the Centre Educatiu Esclat for students about young people's accommodation, the right to decent housing and personal finance management.


Conference at INS Bellvitge

Conference at the INS Bellvitge institute for 4th year of ESO, 1st and 2nd year of Batxillerat students about young people's accommodation and the right to decent housing.


Presence at the construction of new housing in La Florida.

We attended the inauguration of new housing in La Florida, in collaboration with the PAH, to raise awareness and denounce the lack of social housing in the city.


Informative event in collaboration with La Caixa

An informative event was held at the Bellvitge promenade in collaboration with La Caixa and the Spanish Committee ofTools about young people's accommodation and the right to decent housing.


Calling for strikes and demonstrations for solutions to the housing crisis.

Since 2018, we have called for strikes and demonstrations to demand solutions to the housing crisis. In collaboration with the PAH, we have organized actions to raise awareness and denounce the lack of social housing in the city.
